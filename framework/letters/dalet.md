**Position:** 4 of 22 | **Gematria:** 4 | **Unicode:** U+05D3

## Proposed Meaning

**Intersection**

Embodies the profound juncture where the ethereal and tangible realms converge. It captures the moment of creation when a thought or intention crystallizes into tangible form.

**Semantic field:** intersection, door, pathway, threshold, convergence, access, material-immaterial, opening
