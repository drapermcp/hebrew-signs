# Ayin (ע) — Perception

**Position:** 16 of 22 | **Gematria:** 70 | **Unicode:** U+05E2

## Proposed Meaning

**Perception**

Symbolizes the multifaceted nature of perception. It captures the profound ability to perceive realities that lie beyond the evident.

**Semantic field:** perception, eye, seeing, insight, watchfulness, witness, experience, understanding
