# Tav (ת) — Mark

**Position:** 22 of 22 | **Gematria:** 400 | **Unicode:** U+05EA

## Proposed Meaning

**Mark**

Not merely a destination but a symbolic mark on our endless journey of exploration and discovery of truth. It signifies our highest desire, which is the infinite.

**Semantic field:** mark, sign, covenant, completion, truth, seal, cross, destination
