# Gimel (ג) — Causation

**Position:** 3 of 22 | **Gematria:** 3 | **Unicode:** U+05D2

## Proposed Meaning

**Causation**

Encapsulates the intricate dance of causality that governs the universe. It underscores the principle that every action, thought, or occurrence is intrinsically linked to preceding causes and subsequent effects.

**Semantic field:** causation, movement, journey, cause-effect, growth, lifting, carrying, reward
