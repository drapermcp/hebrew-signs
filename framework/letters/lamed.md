## Proposed Meaning

**Learning**

Emblematic of the intrinsic human quest for understanding and enlightenment. It underscores the pivotal role of guidance in our lives, whether leading or being led.

**Semantic field:** learning, teaching, guidance, toward, directing, goad, knowledge, instruction
