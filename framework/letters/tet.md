**Position:** 9 of 22 | **Gematria:** 9 | **Unicode:** U+05D8

## Proposed Meaning

**Harvest**

Represents the profound satisfaction and fulfillment derived from seeing one's efforts bear fruit. It emphasizes the reward that ensues after prolonged dedication and stewardship.

**Semantic field:** harvest, good, fruit, yield, benefit, realization, potential, reward
