# Aleph (א) — Authority

**Position:** 1 of 22 | **Gematria:** 1 | **Unicode:** U+05D0

---

## Proposed Meaning

**Authority**

As the inaugural letter, Aleph stands as a symbol of authority and the driving force behind all beginnings. It represents the inherent power and influence that comes with being the originator or primary force.

**Semantic field:** authority, origin, beginning, divine, leadership, first, primacy, source
