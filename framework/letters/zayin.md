# Zayin (ז) — Intervention

**Position:** 7 of 22 | **Gematria:** 7 | **Unicode:** U+05D6

## Proposed Meaning

**Intervention**

Encapsulates the profound interplay between predetermined paths and the power of individual agency. It emphasizes the moment when an individual steps in to alter a course or change an outcome.

**Semantic field:** intervention, weapon, cutting, sustenance, redirection, agency, action, time
