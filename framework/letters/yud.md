# Yud (י) — Desire and Action

**Position:** 10 of 22 | **Gematria:** 10 | **Unicode:** U+05D9

---

## Proposed Meaning

**Desire and Action**

Stands as a testament to the potency of desire and the dynamic expression of that longing through purposeful action. It represents the journey from desire to realization, from thought to action.

**Semantic field:** desire, hand, action, power, purpose, creative, reaching, making
