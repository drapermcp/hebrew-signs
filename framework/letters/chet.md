# Chet (ח) — Possession

**Position:** 8 of 22 | **Gematria:** 8 | **Unicode:** U+05D7

## Proposed Meaning

**Possession**

Signifies the profound responsibilities and privileges that come with possession. It emphasizes the deeper understanding and consciousness behind such actions.

**Semantic field:** possession, life, fence, enclosure, taking, living, vitality, grace
