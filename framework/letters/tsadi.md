## Proposed Meaning

**Righteousness**

Carries profound symbolism related to the principles of righteousness and moral order. It serves as a symbol for "The Way" or the path leading to absolute truth.

**Semantic field:** righteousness, justice, way, moral-order, truth, right-path, purity, hunt
