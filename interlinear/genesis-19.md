### Recurring Patterns

| Dynamic Pattern | Occurrences | Words |
|-----------------|-------------|-------|
| Direction → Conjunction → Materialization | 15 | and Lot, Lot, in Lot, with Lot |
| Origination → Flow → Headship | 14 | and he said, and they said, saying, and she said |
| Origination → Containment → Agency | 11 | our father, from our father, their father, her father |

| Origination → Intensification → Headship | 8 | who, which, who/which/that, that |
| Summons → Conjunction → Flow | 7 | the place, arise, arise/get up, and in her rising up |
| Agency → Revelation → Conjunction | 7 | YHWH/LORD, the LORD, YHWH, and Yahweh |
| Revelation → Conjunction → Origination | 7 | she/it, he, that, she |
| Origination → Headship → Alignment | 6 | to the ground, the earth, land, the land |

### Key Observations

1. **יהי / ויהי** ("let there be" / "and it was") — Agency → Revelation → Agency. The act of creation is an act that reveals and acts again. The hand makes visible, the hand completes.
2. **אור** ("light") — Origination → Conjunction → Headship. Light originates, joins, and leads. It is the first thing to establish presence through connection.
3. **אלהים** ("God") — Origination → Direction → Revelation. The divine name dynamically reads: that which originates, directs toward, and makes visible.
4. **ברא** ("created") — Containment → Headship → Origination. Creation is enclosing that leads toward establishing new presence. Form given to lead into being.
5. **טוב** ("good") — Materialization → Conjunction → Containment. Goodness is what emerges physically, joins together, and takes form. Wholeness materialized.
6. **ערב / בקר** ("evening / morning") — Perception→Headship→Containment / Containment→Summons→Headship. Evening: seeing that leads into enclosure. Morning: from enclosure, a summons that leads forward.
7. **את** (object marker) — Origination → Completion. Aleph to Tav, first to last. The object marker dynamically spans the entire alphabet.

### Known Limitations

- **שמים** ("heavens") is decomposed as ש+מים (Intensification + root מים). The root extraction treats ש as a prefix. The actual etymology of שמים is debated; this decomposition is heuristic.
- **Verb stems** (binyanim) are detected via MorphHB morphological tagging. Non-Qal verbs show a stem label (e.g., [causative] for Hiphil) before the root dynamics.
- **Suffix labeling** is approximate. Some multi-letter suffixes are labeled generically.
- This is a **raw dynamic reading**, not a polished translation. The dynamics show structural relationships; readable English rendering is a separate step.
